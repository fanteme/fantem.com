OOMI Smart Home System--Security mode

The security of home, the safety of our loved ones, has always been our desire.

User can view the images of closed-circuit monitoring points indoors and outdoors at any time and anywhere, or check videos that occurred earlier through local memory cards or cloud recordings by Touch or mobile phone. Once an intruder is discovered, the Security system will be triggered and an automatic alarm will be issued to notify the owner or relevant security personnel in time to prevent illegal or criminal activities from happening, protecting the interests of the individual. Besides, it also includes other functions such as fire and gas leakage monitoring, and emergency alarms special for the elderly and children.

Burglar alarm -- D&W Sensor

Door & Window Sensor will notify you that someone is entering your home through mobile phone and tigger Siren to sound that alarm when it senses the door or window is opened under Security mode.

Intelligent safeguarding -- MultiSensor (6 in 1/3 in 1)

Built-in motion sensor with a maximum 120° detecting range of 5 meters, sparing no movements in your home.

Safety steward - Siren

Sound and light alarm, notifying you at once and scaring away intruders.

Intimate assistant - Water Sensor

Sound warning alarm when water level reaches preset level or water leak happens, notifying user through phone and turning on smart manipulator to turn off water valve if related automation is triggered.

Safety steward - Smart Smoke Sensor

Trigger alarm and inform user through mobile phone once smoke is detected, warning user to take related action to protect the home.

Safety steward - Smart Gas Sensor

Sound alarm and inform user through mobile phone when flammable gas is detected, meanwhile trigger related devices if presetting has been done.

One-button operation - Smart Security Control

Set Disarm, Home, Leave mode through one button, supporting grouping and linkages among door lock, door sensor, motion sensor, gas sensor and camera, etc. Cut off unnecessary power and trigger security system at the time you leave home and disarm you home once you are back.
