Smart Villa

OOMI Big House System is special for smart mansion. By localization management or remote management through cloud platform, it remains the overall smart control of health, voice, security, lighting, appliances, entertainment and environment. Adopting the unique, powerful cascade system which allows multi-gateway cascade and cross-gateway scene interaction, the System is stable and reliable and completely solves the problem of wireless product application in villa. It can meet the needs of all family members as both centralized control and hierarchical control are applicable. In terms of function and performance, "people-oriented" concept has been throughout, enabling people to fully experience the comfort, security, informatization and other aspects brought by the smart home system to daily life.
