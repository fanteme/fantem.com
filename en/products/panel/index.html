OOMI Touch Panel(S series)

OOMI Touch Panel

Oomi Touch Panel (S Series) includes single switch, dual switch, quad switch and variance switch. It is base on Z-wave wireless application protocol. It recognizes action controls such as tap, short-press and long-press to control whatever devices connected to the smart home.

Soft Touch with Light Indicator

Highly sensitive capacitive touch makes the device respond in milliseconds, really fast. Specially designed pin light LED with soft white illumination that doesn’t glare in the dark, it is easy to find and doesn’t intrude with your sleep.

Finely Crafted Aesthetics

Tempered glass surface，Scratch-proof and shatter-resistant

Dust-resistant, effectively prevent small particles.

Water proof , control even with wet hands

PC110 materials flame retardant, heat & pressure resisting, anti- aging

Personalized Scene Setting

Oomi App lets you control remotely and monitor status on your smart phones. Adapt the Touch Panels to your daily routine by setting device control or scene mode with a simple short press and long press.

Simple Control, Smart Life

No rewiring needed just replace normal flip switches with touch panel and connect to Oomi gateway to operate with other Oomi devices. The minimalist craftsmanship is thoughtfully designed with polish chromium plating, scratch-proof glass, curved edge perfectly fitted for luxury and elegance style.

Intelligent Multi-Operations

Automating your smart touch panels to turn on when you get home is an added ease to a long stressful day from work.

Meanwhile before you even wake up in the morning, smart dimming panel will turn the lights on at regular interval and gradually eases you up from sleep, making a really good morning!

Your Smart Life Doesn’t Stop Here!

With Touch Panels you can control your switches wirelessly and remotely while retaining the traditional functions and what’s more you can add new smart scene control function! Because it has NFC contacts printed on the protective film layer you can control it with Oomi Touch. The back lights indicator on the panels is easy to see at a glance, different colors meant different setting.
