OOMI In-Wall Switch

Add smart control to existing switches inside the home. Small enough to wire in most electrical boxes, In-Wall Switch or Dual In-Wall Switch provides remote control and automation of existing recessed or mounted fixtures where other smart bulbs aren’t practical or desired.
