OOMI Curtain Controller

OOMI Fantem Curtain

OOMI Curtain Controller helps user control their curtain remotely and wireless. User can control their curtain to stop at particular point to match their home automation.

一键开关窗帘

定时设置

联动操控

一键开启窗帘 开合自如

随开随关，控制自如。只需轻轻一按OOMI智能场景面板，即可打开或者关闭窗帘，开合自如，享受科技的温暖。

Timing Setting Automation

OOMI Curtain Controller can interact with other OOMI devices under user setting that based on user habit or preference.

Control at Will

Through OOMI Mote, OOMI Touch or OOMI App, user can control curtain at their will, managing curtain to stop at any position they want.

Smart Control Releases Your Hands

OOMI Curtain Controller supports voice control. User can talk to intelligent speakers such as Tmall Genie or Xiaodu to control their curtain.

High Quality Guarantee

Metal as the motor’s shell, PC material covers the controller, OOMI Curtain Control possesses multiple types and has been qualified with CCC certification, guaranteeing you both high quality and great safety.
