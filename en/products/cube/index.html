OOMI Cube

The whole home controller

OOMI Cube is the brains of the OOMI Smart Home System. Besides being the ultimate home manager, it also has a security camera with night vision, four environmental sensors, and a 360° IR blaster to control legacy TV and entertainment systems as well as other infrared controlled devices.

OOMI Cube，

Five functions to better your life.

01 Security camera with night vision

高清红外夜视，再黑也能拍清楚

Secure your home safety 24 hours a day,Even in darkness can nothing skip the camera.

User can check the entire home situation in real time

Can be informed when burglary is detected under Security or Leave mode.

02 Temperature and humidity sensors

If extreme temperature or extreme humidity is detected, OOMI Cube can automatically turn on related temperature-adjusting or humidity-adjusting appliances to rectify the entire home condition.

全天湿度
全天温度

联动智能设备 创造舒适生活环境

拒绝湿度失衡

当传感器检测到室内环境过于干燥/潮湿时，Cube智能家庭网关可通过联动智能插座，打开与其相连的加湿器/除湿器，将室内湿度调至适宜水平。

  • 检测空气质量

  • 分析数据

  • 联动插座设备

  • 开启加湿器

拒绝极端室温

当温度过高/过低时，传感器可联动OOMI智能空调红外转换器自动开启空调制冷/制热模式，时刻维持恒温舒适的家居环境。

  • 检测室内温度

  • 分析数据

  • 联动遥控设备

  • 开启空调

03 Brightness sensor

Detecting your home brightness, having lights illuminate as it gets dark or turn off when nobody around.

智能识别家居环境光线明暗度，可结合预设的智能场景自动调节灯光亮度。

04 Motion sensor

Detecting movements of things and/or creatures, showing related data on one’s mobile phone.

05 Speaker and microphone

Two-way voice intercom, allowing you to communicate with your home member whenever and wherever you are.
