Fantem Technologies (Shenzhen) Co., Ltd is a joint venture founded in 2011 by American company Aeon Labs LLC and Chinese company YITOA. For over a decade Fantem has specialized in the research and development, production and sales of smart home products for the global market.

Possessing an American Silicon Valley firm providing advanced technologies, with over 100 patents and an anual output of more than one million smart home product sets, Fantem has earned great reputation among its millions of users across the world for the products' state of art technology, super practical functions and even the fashionable outlook design. OOMI never limits itself to the field of smart home, it is applicable to many other fields including hotels and offices.
